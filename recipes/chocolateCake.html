Chocolate Cake

Description

A delicious chocolate cake that can be made in just over half an hour!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flower
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon espresso powder
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Prepare one 9 x 13 baking dish by spraying with baking spray or buttering and lightly flouring.
  2. Whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, salt and espresso powder in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add milk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla to mixture and mix together until well combined. Care fully stir the boling water into the cake batter and pour into the prepared 9 x 13 dish.
  4. Back for 30 - 35 minutes, until a toothpick or cake tester inserted in the center comes out clean.
  5. Remove from oven and allow to cool completely.
